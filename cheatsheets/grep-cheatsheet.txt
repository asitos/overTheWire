3. grep (The Filter)

You already know grep, but its flags are what make it powerful for CTFs.


    Case-insensitive search:
    Bash

    grep -i "password" file.txt


    Invert match (show everything EXCEPT the keyword):
    Bash

    grep -v "garbage_data" file.txt


    Show lines around the match (Context):
    Bash

    grep -C 2 "keyword" file.txt

    (Shows the matching line, plus 2 lines above and 2 lines below. You can also use -A 2 for After or -B 2 for Before).
