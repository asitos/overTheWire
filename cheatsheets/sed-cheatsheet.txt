2. sed (The Stream Editor)

sed is perfect for modifying text on the fly. You'll mostly use it for finding and replacing (substitution).


    Basic Find and Replace:
    Bash

    sed 's/find_this/replace_with_this/' file.txt


    Delete a specific word entirely:
    Bash

    sed 's/annoying_word//g' file.txt

    (The g at the end stands for "global," meaning it will replace every instance on the line, not just the first one).


    Print only specific lines (e.g., lines 10 to 15):
    Bash

    sed -n '10,15p' file.txt
